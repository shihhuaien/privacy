資料收集

  • 使用行為分析
  • 廣告追蹤資料

App 內無帳號系統、無登入功能、無任何形式的雲端服務。

裝置權限

DuoRatio 會請求以下裝置權限，僅用於核心錄影功能：

  • 相機 — 錄製影片（必要）
  • 麥克風 — 錄製影片音訊（必要）
  • 相簿 — 將錄製完成的影片儲存至你的相簿（必要）

這些權限僅在裝置本地使用，不會將任何資料傳送至外部伺服器。

第三方服務

DuoRatio 不整合任何第三方分析、廣告或追蹤服務。App 不包含 SDK 或程式碼會將資料傳送至第三方。

影片儲存

所有錄製的影片僅儲存在你的裝置相簿中。DuoRatio 不會上傳、備份或以任何方式將影片傳輸至外部。錄影期間的暫存檔案在儲存完成後會立即刪除。

Overview

DuoRatio is a fully offline camera recording app. We do not collect, store, or transmit any user data. Your videos stay on your device and nowhere else.

In short: we collect nothing.

Data Collection

DuoRatio does not collect any personal information, including but not limited to:

  • Name, email address, or phone number
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics
  • Advertising tracking data

The app has no account system, no login, and no cloud services of any kind.

Device Permissions

DuoRatio requests the following device permissions, used solely for core recording functionality:

  • Camera — to record video (required)
  • Microphone — to record audio with video (required)
  • Photo Library — to save recorded videos to your library (required)

These permissions are used locally on your device only. No data is transmitted to any external server.

Third-Party Services

DuoRatio does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app contains no SDKs or code that transmit data to third parties.

Video Storage

All recorded videos are stored exclusively in your device's photo library. DuoRatio does not upload, back up, or transmit videos externally in any way. Temporary files created during recording are deleted immediately after saving is complete.

Children's Privacy

DuoRatio is not designed for children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

常見問題

  • 為什麼我的裝置不支援雙鏡頭模式？

    雙鏡頭同步錄影需要支援 AVCaptureMultiCamSession 的機型（iPhone XS 或更新）。如果你的裝置不支援，App 會自動切換至單鏡頭模式，同樣可以輸出兩種比例的影片。

  • 影片儲存在哪裡？

    所有影片都儲存在你的裝置相簿中。DuoRatio 不會上傳任何檔案到雲端。

  • 錄影時手機發燙怎麼辦？

    雙鏡頭同時錄影會消耗較多資源，屬正常現象。如果裝置過熱，App 會主動提示你。建議在通風良好的環境下使用，並避免長時間連續錄影。

  • 支援哪些影片格式與解析度？

    支援 MOV 與 MP4 格式，解析度可選 4K 或 1080p，幀率支援 24 / 30 / 60 fps。可在設定中調整。

  • 如何恢復購買？

    DuoRatio 為一次性付費 App，購買後即可永久使用。如需重新下載，請使用購買時的 Apple ID 至 App Store 重新下載即可，不會再次收費。

FAQ

  • Why doesn't my device support dual-lens mode?

    Simultaneous dual-lens recording requires a device that supports AVCaptureMultiCamSession (iPhone XS or later). If your device doesn't support it, the app will automatically switch to single-lens mode, which still outputs videos in both aspect ratios.

  • Where are my videos saved?

    All videos are saved to your device's photo library. DuoRatio does not upload any files to the cloud.

  • My phone gets warm during recording. Is this normal?

    Dual-lens simultaneous recording uses more resources, which is expected. The app will alert you if the device overheats. We recommend recording in a well-ventilated area and avoiding extended recording sessions.

  • What video formats and resolutions are supported?

    DuoRatio supports MOV and MP4 formats, with 4K or 1080p resolution, and 24 / 30 / 60 fps frame rates. You can adjust these in Settings.

  • How do I restore my purchase?

    DuoRatio is a one-time purchase. Once bought, it's yours forever. To re-download, simply use the same Apple ID on the App Store — you won't be charged again.
